Organizational Structure & Hierarchy

Our organization is built on a tiered structure that ensures both effective leadership and broad community engagement. Each group plays a vital role in advancing our mission in LMICs.

Founding Members

The foundational body responsible for setting the vision, mission, and long-term strategic direction of the organization.

Executive Board & Advisors

Responsible for daily governance, financial oversight, and ensuring strategic goals are met. Includes Executive Directors and key advisors.

Committee Chairs & Regional Leads

Leads specific initiatives (e.g., Education, Research) and regional chapter activities, driving program implementation.

Members

The core community of dedicated healthcare professionals, researchers, and supporters who contribute actively to our projects and mission.

Volunteers

Individuals who dedicate their time and expertise to specific tasks, events, and fieldwork essential for local support and program delivery.

Donors, Friends, Collaborators & Supporters

Essential external partners providing financial support, resources, and outreach, crucial for sustaining and expanding our work.
