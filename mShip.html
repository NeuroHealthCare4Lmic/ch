  • Individual membership categories based on World Bank categories according to income countries (Higher/ Middle/ Lower income countries) list.

What membership benefits we offer

  • Input in policy making and directions. Leadership roles and voting rights.
  • Networking, updates of organizational activities, career guidance.
  • Exclusive assignments based on their professional, educational, dedication and interest base issues.
  • Participation in formatting challenges, writing publications, presenting at forums.
  • Preference for any volunteer activity a member wants to participate or create in the lines of organizational mission.
  • Various discounts at organization or partner organizational initiatives, workshop, courses, conferences.
  • Recognition and awards.
